## Welcome

Welcome to the documentation! This site is your central resource for guides, tutorials, and reference material. Whether you are just getting started or looking for advanced configuration details, you will find everything you need here.

## How to Navigate

Use the **sidebar** on the left to browse articles by category. On desktop, you will also see a **table of contents** on the right side of each article for quick navigation within a page.

### Search

The **search bar** at the top of every page lets you find articles instantly. It uses full-text search in production builds and a JSON-based fallback during development.

### Categories

Content is organized into the following categories:

- **Getting Started** — Installation, setup, and first steps.
- **Guides** — Step-by-step walkthroughs for common tasks.
- **Features** — Details on what this site offers out of the box.
- **Reference** — Technical specifications and configuration options.

## What's Next?

If this is your first time here, start with the [Installation](/docs/getting-started/installation) guide to get up and running. Then explore the [Configuration](/docs/getting-started/configuration) article to customize the site for your needs.

## Need Help?

If you cannot find what you are looking for, try using the search bar or browse the categories in the sidebar. You can also check the [Deployment](/docs/reference/deployment) guide when you are ready to go live.
